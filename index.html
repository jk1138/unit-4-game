Welcome to the "Crystal Mines". You will have to match a randomly generated sparkle score throughout the game. This can be acheieved by "mining" (clicking) on the crystals shown below. You will add a specific amount of points to your total score. But be carful, each newround will generate a different sparkle score! You win the game by matching your sparkle score to the randomly generated number, but you will lose the game if your total sparkle score goes above the random number. Good luck!
